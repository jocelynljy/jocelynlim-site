Facilitation

Workshops that turn a group of strangers into people who actually talk to each other.

Hosting is holding a room. Facilitation is designing one. My job is to create the conditions where people feel safe enough to be honest — and then get out of the way so the good stuff can happen.

Most recently I've been working with architects and the Singapore Children's Society to co-design a community space in Yishun, and running leadership workshops in schools. I've also facilitated youth programmes where the real win isn't the activity on the slide — it's the kid who speaks up for the very first time.

Safe, playful, intentional. That's the whole method.

Facilitation is me shaping connection — building the room so the realness can happen.
