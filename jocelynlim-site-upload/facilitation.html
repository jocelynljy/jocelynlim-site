Facilitation

Workshops that turn a group of strangers into people who actually talk to each other.

Hosting is holding a room. Facilitation is designing one. My job is to create the conditions where people feel safe enough to be honest — and then get out of the way so the good stuff can happen.

Most of my facilitation is with students and schools: leadership and reflection workshops, and youth programmes where the real win isn't the activity on the slide — it's the student who speaks up for the very first time.

Safe, playful, intentional. That's the whole method.

🔒
Because most of this work is with schools and young people, I keep real session photos private under NDA. The images on this page are AI-generated illustrations of the kind of space I try to create — not actual participants.
Facilitation is me shaping connection — building the room so the realness can happen.
