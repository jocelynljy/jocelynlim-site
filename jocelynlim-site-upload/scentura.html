Scentura

Helping brands speak through scent — bringing a human, sensory layer to cold, digital commerce.

So much of how we buy and eat now happens through a screen. You scroll, you tap, it arrives. Efficient — but a little soulless. Scentura is my answer to that.

It's scent as a way for brands — especially cold-food businesses — to create a moment people can actually feel. A digital storefront can't smell like anything. A product that arrives at your door can. That gap is the whole opportunity.

It started as a question about human experience in a digitalised world, and it's becoming something real: Scentura was recognised as a Voter's Choice at a Growing Enterprises showcase, pitched at Enterprise Singapore, and is being featured in NUS graduation news.

Scentura is me making the digital feel human again — one scent at a time.
