      Why I made this

      I host for a living, so first impressions are kind of my obsession.

      As an emcee and facilitator, I walk into rooms full of strangers, and my whole job is to make them feel something good, fast. Somewhere along the way I got hooked on the question this quiz asks: what do people pick up from you in the first second, before you have said a word? This is my playful take on it, built on the two things psychologists say we read first in anyone, warmth and boldness.

      It is a sneaky-good icebreaker

      Do it as a group and watch the room light up. Everyone gets a character, and suddenly people are comparing, laughing, and arguing over who is really The Commander. It is the exact kind of warm-up I run for teams and socials: low pressure, a little revealing, over in two minutes. Try it at your next dinner, offsite, or first date.
