Now: just graduated & building in public
the why

Lots of side quests. One reason.

On paper, my week makes no sense. One day I'm holding a mic in front of a room. The next I'm rewriting a pitch, prepping for a facilitation session, or cutting pipe cleaners into petals at my kitchen table. People keep telling me to pick one thing.

I'm graduating into a lot of noise — the job market is brutal, AI is coming for the work. But here's the quieter thing I believe: there are still some things AI can't take from us. The comfort of making things with people, by people, for people? That.

That's the thread connecting it all: meet people, go deeper, and stay connected.
