letsbloom

Handmade flowers, craft workshops, and a community that meets to make things — and stays for the conversation.

letsbloom started with pipe cleaners and a stubborn belief: people are happier when they make something with their hands, next to other people. I fold flowers that don't wilt — little handmade blooms you can gift, keep, or build yourself from a DIY kit.

But the flowers are really an excuse. The point is the table they're made at — a low-pressure, screen-down space where young adults meet, craft, and end up in the kind of conversations you just don't get from a group chat. Some sessions even happen over a video call, halfway across the world.

Single stems. Bouquets you choose. DIY kits. Workshops online and in person. And one day — a flower market worth travelling for.

letsbloom is me growing connection — slowly, by hand, in real life.
