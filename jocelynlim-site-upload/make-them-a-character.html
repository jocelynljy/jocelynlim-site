Make Them
a Character

I turned my friends into tiny hand-drawn characters, based on their personalities. Then I put them on postcards, birthday cards, even cakes. Here's the exact recipe, free. :)

Okay, this is one of my favourite things I've ever made. 🤍

One day I turned each of my friends into a little character, drawn entirely from their personality. The dancer got a twerk. The chaos gremlin got a giant hammer. The hopeless romantic got heart-eyes and a "muah muah muah."

Then I went a bit overboard (as one does). I printed them as postcards and wrote everyone a handwritten letter. Turned them into birthday cards. We even printed them onto cakes. Watching someone realise the tiny character is them? Best reaction ever.

So here's the whole recipe. It's free. Steal it, make your people, and go a little overboard too.

Turning a real person into a drawing

The fun part is reading their personality, then exaggerating it.

◆
A bit of a menace? Give them a giant weapon, or a split "angel and devil" face.
◆
Always dancing? Use a big dynamic pose: a twerk, a spin, a bounce.
◆
Always laughing? Eye-smiles (^^) and hands squishing their cheeks.
◆
Obsessed with someone or something? Heart-eyes, maybe a tiny bit of drool.
◆
Effortlessly chill? Half-lidded eyes and a "heh" smirk.
◆
One signature habit? Float 1 or 2 little "totem" objects around them.

Two more tips: only colour the hair and a prop or two (leave the clothes white so they pop), and always say "cream paper background" so it doesn't come out clinical white.
